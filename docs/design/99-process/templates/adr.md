## Decision

What we're doing, in a sentence or two. The first thing the reader should learn.

## Reasoning

Open with a concrete example that grounds the problem, then build up to the
decision one step at a time, explaining each idea as it becomes relevant. A reader
who started with no context should finish understanding both what we chose and why
it's the right call.

## Consequences

What this commits us to — the trade-offs we accept, what it enables, what it rules
out.

## Alternatives considered

The options we weighed and set aside, and why. Last on purpose: the reader meets
them only after they understand the decision.

## Related

Durable in-repo references only — related domain docs, principles, or the ADR this
supersedes. Never tickets or milestones.
